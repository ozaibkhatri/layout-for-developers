News Feed

See what's new in your network.

The news feed on Facebook is a constantly updated stream of content that appears on a user's main page or home screen. It is a central feature of the Facebook platform that displays posts, photos, videos, and other types of content shared by the user's friends, pages they follow, and groups they are part of.
